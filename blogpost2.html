A Café

by Sophie ✨
Facebook Instagram

Hidden in the serene hills of Siquijor, Lambojon Terraces – La Canopee offers a quiet escape into nature’s embrace. Perched on a netted hammock beside a rustic wooden platform, I found myself surrounded by a symphony of leaves and filtered sunlight. The forest wraps around you like a gentle hug, and the soft sway of the hammock makes time feel slower, sweeter. It’s the kind of place where you sip your drink slowly, breathe deeply, and let the calm seep into your bones—a perfect blend of café charm and nature’s healing touch.

Description

A tranquil moment at Lambojon Terraces – La Canopee in Siquijor, where a hammock nestles beside a wooden deck in the heart of the forest, inviting peaceful reflection amid lush greenery.
